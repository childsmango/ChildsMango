ChildsMango - Farming Practices

Our mangoes are nurtured without synthetic chemicals. Natural compost and traditional pest control techniques are used for safe, healthy fruits. The principle components used are Pumpkins - Banana - Papaya - Jaggery in the ratio of 1:1:1:1 being mixed and left to ferment for 21 days with regular monitoring. This mixture gives us the essential micronutrients any plant could need.

Our practices include composting, natural pest repellents, and intercropping to promote biodiversity. The principle components used are Neem - Nilavembu@Siriyanangai - Morinda Citrifolia - Calotropis gigantea - Aloevera - Mother Soil in the ratio of 3:3:3:3:1:0.1 being mixed and left to ferment for 7 days with regular monitoring. This mixture gives a natural shield against pests.

By using intercropping techniques, we can create a harmonious environment for the mango trees and encourage the growth of other beneficial plants.
