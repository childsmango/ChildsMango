Our Mango Varieties

Alphonso Mango

Known as the king of mangoes, famous for its rich, creamy texture and sweet flavor with saffron undertones.

Season: April - June

Kesar Mango

Saffron-colored flesh with a distinct aroma and sweet flavor. Often called the queen of mangoes.

Season: May - July

Banganapalle

Large, oblong-shaped mango with thin skin and fiberless, sweet flesh. A popular export variety.

Season: April - July

Totapuri

Distinct beak-shaped tip with tangy-sweet flavor. Excellent for making pickles and chutneys.

Season: June - August

Neelam

Small to medium-sized with sweet, fiberless flesh. Known for its long shelf life.

Season: June - August

Dasheri

Medium-sized with sweet, aromatic flesh and minimal fiber. A popular North Indian variety.

Season: June - July
